Roomify Platform
================

Roomify for Accommodations - it can scale from a single vacation rental website to full multi-property / multi-owner for vacation rentals, hotels and B&Bs. It is built on top of our unique, open-source, Booking and Availability Management framework: :ref:`BAT <BAT>`.

Rooms DocumentationÂ¶

This documentation refers to the Rooms 1.11 module .

Rooms is a set of Drupal modules designed to enable individual holiday homes, B&Bs and hotels to define what is available for booking (whether it is the entire building or rooms within a building), manage the availability and pricing and integrate with Drupal Commerce to handle payment.

Roomify Platform
================
The *Roomify Platform* is the next-generation solution for online booking and availability management from Roomify. It is built on top of our unique, open-source, Booking and Availability Management framework: :ref:`BAT`.

The Roomify Platform allows users to manage availability, pricing and booking, within a single platform, for a multiple range of scenarios.
